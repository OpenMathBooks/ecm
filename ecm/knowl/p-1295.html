The partition \((\lambda_1,\lambda_2,\ldots, \lambda_n)\) is called the conjugate of the partition \((\gamma_1,\gamma_2,\ldots, \gamma_m)\) if we obtain the Young diagram of one from the Young diagram of the other by flipping one around the line with slope -1 that extends the diagonal of the top left square.
