The above discussion is meant to explain why it is reasonable to believe that induction is a valid proof technique. 2  Once we believe induction works, we can just use it. That means just proving two things: \(P(0)\) and for all \(n \ge 0\text{,}\) \(P(n) \imp P(n+1)\text{.}\) We call \(P(0)\) the base case and the implication the inductive case.
