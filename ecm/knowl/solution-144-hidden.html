To solve for \(C_n\text{,}\) write

\begin{align*} \sum_{n=0}^\infty C_nx^n \amp= 1+\sum_{n=1}^\infty\sum_{i=1}^nC_{i-1}C_{n-i}x^n\\ \sum_{n=0}^\infty C_nx^n \amp= 1+x\sum_{n=1}^\infty\sum_{i=1}^nC_{i-1}x^{i-1}C_{n-i}x^{n-i}\\ \sum_{n=0}^\infty C_nx^n \amp= 1+x\sum_{i=1}^\infty C_{i-1}x^{i-1}\sum_{j=0}^\infty C_{j}x^{j}\\ y \amp= 1 + xy^2\text{.} \end{align*}

This gives us \(xy^2-y+1=0\text{,}\) and solving for \(y\) by the quadratic formula gives us \(y=\frac{1\pm \sqrt{1-4x}}{2x}\text{.}\)
