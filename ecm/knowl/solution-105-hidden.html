\(x^3+3x^4+4x^5+3x^6+x^7\text{.}\) There is one way to choose three pieces of fruit, there are three ways to choose four pieces, four ways to chose 5 pieces, three ways to choose 6 pieces , and there is one way to choose 7 pieces of fruit. The coefficient of \(x^n\) is the number of ways to choose \(n\) pieces of fruit.
