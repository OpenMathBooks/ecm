Solution 176.1.
\begin{align*} \sum_{i=1}^\infty a_ix^i =\amp 3\sum_{i=1}^\infty a_{i-1}x^i +\sum_{i=1}^\infty 2^ix^i\\ \sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i -a_0 =\amp 3x\sum_{i=1}^\infty a_{i-1}x^{i-1} +\sum_{i=0}^\infty (2x)^i -1\\ \sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i -a_0 =\amp 3x\sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i +\frac{1}{1-2x} -1\\ (1-3x)\sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i =\amp a_0 +\frac{1}{1-2x}-1\\ \sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i =\amp \frac{a_0-1}{1-3x}+\frac{1}{(1-2x)(1-3x)}\\ \sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i =\amp (a_0-1)\sum_{i=0}^\infty 3^ix^i +\sum_{i=0}^\infty 2^ix^i\sum_{j=0}^\infty 3^jx^j \end{align*}

But

\begin{align*} \sum_{i=0}^\infty 2^ix^i\sum_{j=0}^\infty 3^jx^j\amp= \sum_{k=0}^\infty \sum_{i=0}^k 2^i3^{k-i}x^k= \sum_{k=0}^\infty3^kx^k \sum_{i=0}^k \frac{2^i}{3^i}\\ \amp=\sum_{k=0}^\infty \frac{1-\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{k+1}}{1-\frac{2}{3}}3^kx^k =\sum_{k=0}^\infty (3^{k+1}-2^{k+1})x^k\text{.} \end{align*}

Substituting this into the equation for \(\sum_{i=0}^\infty a_ix^i\) gives us \(a_i =(a_0+2)3^i -2^{i+1}\text{.}\)
